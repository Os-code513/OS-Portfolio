Breaking Bad

2008, TV-MA , 5 seasons

Breaking Bad is a TV series about Walter White, a high school teacher who turns to making meth after a cancer diagnosis Partnering with former student Jesse Pinkman, Walter's journey into the drug world leads to dangerous consequences, moral struggles, and a battle for survival.

Creator:Vince Gilligan

Cast:Bryan Cranston, Aaron Paul,Anna Gunn, Dean Norris, Betsy Brandt, RJ Mitte, Bob Odenkirk, Jonathan Banks and Giancarlo Esposito
